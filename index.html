So, something about me:

As I said,
"I changed my life, I leart how to code.
And YES I'm a coder now."

I was born in Durres, Albania. At age 5(more or less) my family moved in Greece, for a better future. I studied there, grew up there, and when,let's say, things were good, my dad decided to move to another country, again. This time in Italy, always for the same reason. But things never go as you want, so no better future till now.
Now it's my turn to create a better future.
I always worked as a professional cook, but i knew that this wasn't the right job for me. I knew that I wasn't in the right place. I felt lost.

So I started to learn coding. A huge challenge, that i happily accepted!
But it was and still is hard but it's fine. That's what I wanted to do, I was sure.

I decided to attend a coding bootcamp, I chose Le Wagon, in Milan, and damn that was tough, but it worth it, DEFINITELY.
And here I am now. A junior web developer.
Ready for new challenges!
